For Gmail users: You MUST use an App Password, not your regular Gmail password. View Detailed Instructions
How to create an App Password for Gmail:
  1. Go to your Google Account at myaccount.google.com
  2. Select "Security" from the left navigation panel
  3. Under "Signing in to Google," select "2-Step Verification" (you must have this enabled)
  4. At the bottom of the page, select "App passwords"
  5. Enter a name like "ArrayForms Email Sender"
  6. Click "Create" and copy the 16-character password
  7. Paste that password in the field above (not your regular Gmail password)
