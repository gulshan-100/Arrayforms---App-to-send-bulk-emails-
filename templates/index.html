Data Protection

Your Data is Secure

Your email credentials are only used to send emails and are never stored on our servers. We don't save any of your personal information.

Individual Emails

Each recipient gets their own individual email. Recipients cannot see other email addresses, ensuring privacy.

No History Stored

We don't keep any records of your email campaigns, recipients, or content. Everything is processed in real-time and then forgotten.
