Tips & Tricks

Personalized Emails

Each recipient will receive an individual email and won't see other recipients.

Secure Authentication

Your credentials are only used to send emails and are never stored.

Send Limits

Gmail has limits of 20 emails/hour and 500 emails/day. Consider spacing out larger sends.
